Marketing Strategy Overview

🌐Global Marketing

Standardized approach across multiple international markets with unified brand messaging and strategies.

  • Multi-country presence
  • Standardized messaging
  • Complex coordination
  • Higher investment requirements

🏠Domestic Marketing

Focused approach within a single country or region, leveraging deep local market knowledge.

  • Single market focus
  • Local expertise
  • Simplified operations
  • Lower barrier to entry
